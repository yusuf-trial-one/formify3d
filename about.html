About Me

A curious 13-year-old with a 3D printer and a big idea.

I’m Yusuf — the maker behind Formify 3D, and the person who designed and built this website from scratch.

My name is Yusuf Muhammad — and this entire website was built from scratch by me. Creating a website isn’t easy. It takes patience, dedication and plenty of mistakes, but launching it publicly became one of my biggest achievements.

This whole journey started with one simple spark of curiosity. “What if I made a 3D printing website? I already have a printer… so why not turn this into something greater?” And that thought changed everything for me.

I began searching, learning and experimenting. One day became two. Two became three. And somewhere along the way, the hard work turned into enjoyment — and curiosity grew into Formify 3D.

Outside of 3D printing, I enjoy biking, gaming, reading and discovering new hobbies. And now, this website has become one of my favourite creations — something I’m proud of and constantly improving.

Where this is all going

My goals are bigger than just printing cool objects. I want to learn real skills, grow as a creator, and one day achieve financial freedom — all while having fun on the journey. And here’s the truth: you are the one who decides whether this dream grows or fades. The choice is yours…
