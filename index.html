My Story

Who's Behind Formify 3D?

I'm Yusuf, a Year 8 student at Wymondham High School who loves building things. What started as a hobby with my 3D printer has grown into something bigger.

I'm growing this into a real business to help fund my future university studies — and I donate 25% of my profit to charity supporting people with liver disease.

Every print I make teaches me something new about design, engineering, and entrepreneurship. Plus, it's just really cool to see ideas become real objects!
